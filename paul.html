BIO:
My name is Paul "MEXONE" Vetne, I have been a professional Tattoo Artist for 7 years. I started tattooing in 2005, after completeing a tattoo apprenticeship. I was born in Guadalajara, Mexico and raised in a family of self taught artists who provided encouragement and support for all forms of artistic expression. This allowed me the freedom to have many artistic and cultural experiences. I truely feel that my family's love of art, lead me to become a professional artist.

I attended art school at Andrews University in Southwest Michigan, where I achieved my Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree as a Painter.

I try not to limit myself to one medium. I am fascinated by images on flat surfaces as well as three dimensional and express myself through sculpture, illustration, painting, woodworking, and the urban art of graffiti. I find inspiration in my Mexican Heritage, artists of the Dadaist, Surrealist, and Mexican muralist movements as well as comic books and film.
