BIO:
Hey hows it going? My name is Shaun Stiles. Ive been professionally tattooing for the past three years. I have always been into art and spent most of my teenage years drawing and playing guitar. My brother then started getting tattood and it was all over. We started tattooing each other and were having no success. I decided if I really wanted to do this I need to do it right, I needed to get an apprenticship. It took a while to find a mentor but when I did I quit my job and fucking dove into tattooing. I haven't been tattooing for a long time but I make up for that with heart, drive, and ambition. My favorite thing to tattoo is black and gray, skulls, or anything on the dark side. I also love new school and some traditional. I love to paint, it has become my favorite hobby. I use mainly acrylic paint and I like to draw with charcoal as well. Some of my main inspirations are Disney animation and marvel comics.
